About Us

At Free The Forgotten, we are committed to uplifting every member of our community through compassion, awareness, and action. Based in the vibrant heart of Church End, within the diverse borough of Brent, London, our charity works tirelessly to support individuals from all backgrounds especially those who may feel forgotten or overlooked. Through outreach, advocacy, and hands-on support, we strive to build a future where every voice is heard, every need is acknowledged, and every person is valued with dignity and respect.
